VEHICLE RECALL NOTICE

WARNING

One or more vehicles that you own are under recall.

Vehicle License Plate Registration Date Status

POSSIBLE REASONS FOR THIS RECALL

Vehicles can be recalled for the following reasons:

  • Significant body and visual defects caused by the manufacturer that cannot be easily fixed
  • Cosmetic issues that cause people to see artifacts or flickering when standing nearby
  • Model is oversized and has a polygon or vertices count that exceeds normal limits* (L0 of 200k+).

WHAT DOES THIS MEAN?

You are receiving this notice because you are the registered owner of one or more vehicle(s) listed above. Please review the entire notice letter in full and take the recommended actions.

RECOMMENDED ACTION

Vehicles under recall are subject to a complete removal a minimum of 14 days from the initial recall date. If you are receiving this notice, please return the vehicles listed above to the dealership immediately. This can be done in the purple parking circle behind PDM: 200 Adams Apple Blvd. Currently, the residual value on recalled vehicles is 70% for ones originally purchased as a supporter vehicle, and 50% for non-supporter vehicles. This figure is subject to change at the sole discretion of the recall authority. Failure to return the vehicle within the recommended time frame will result in a complete loss of the vehicle with no recourse for the registered owner.

WHY IS 'X' VEHICLE BEING RECALLED?

Over the past few months, there have been numerous complaints of freezes, frame drops, hangs, stuttering, and other client performance issues in populated areas with a lot of player owned vehicles. These issues are unacceptable and are being addressed with the recall system. One of our top priorities is to provide the smoothest experience for everyone regardless of any hardware considerations. The goal is to slowly reduce the number of problematic vehicles on the road, which will lead to a noticeable improvement in player experience over time. Note that these improvements will be gradual and manifest over the long run, when the number of recalled vehicles gets reduced to zero.

CAN A RECALLED VEHICLE BE TRADED IN FOR ANOTHER VEHICLE AT THE DEALERSHIP?

No. Vehicles that are under recall cannot be exchanged as the issue is with the vehicle model itself being defective or oversized. Recall costs are covered by the federal government and are not issued by the dealership.

DO I NEED SUPPORTER STATUS TO RETURN A RECALLED VEHICLE?

No. Vehicles under recall can be sold back to PDM regardless of supporter status or whether the vehicle was originally purchased as a supporter vehicle. This is done to ensure everyone has a fair chance to return recalled vehicles..

IS THERE ANYTHING ELSE THAT CAN BE DONE INSTEAD?

No. Vehicles that are under recall cannot be registered, sold, or transferred to other players. The recall system was introduced to provide a fair and uniform process for anyone who owns a recalled vehicle. Do NOT attempt to contact the recall authority or any government officials, there is nothing that can be done on an individual basis.

* See https://www.modding-forum.com/guide/1-lod-models/ for more information.

WHAT IS COMMUNITY SERVICE?

The staff team can, at their discretion, issue community service to players found in violation of the server rules.

WHO HAS THE AUTHORITY TO ISSUE COMMUNITY SERVICE?

Community service can be issued by any staff member (regardless of job) or a whitelisted job holder, provided they are on duty and within the limits of their job. If you are receiving this notice, your sentence was issued by the staff team -- NOT by a whitelisted job. Community service issued by a whitelisted job is considered a non-staff punishment and does not appear on your record.

HOW DO I COMPLETE MY SERVICE?

Players who are issued community service must complete actions by sweeping the beach. One month is equal to one action of service. Find the appropriate locations and follow the instructional message.

I BELIEVE THIS WAS INCORRECTLY ISSUED. WHAT RECOURSE DO I HAVE?

If you believe this was issued in error, open a #create-ticket on Discord (discord.gg/cityofla) and explain your reasoning. Do NOT place assists regarding your community service or you will be assist blacklisted. Copy and paste the entire contents of this notice when submitting an appeal to reduce or remove your sentence.
